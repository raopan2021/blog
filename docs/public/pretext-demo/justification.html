Justification Algorithms

三种段落对齐算法的对比：CSS 默认、贪心连字符、Knuth-Plass。观察"河流"（white rivers）和词间间距的分布。调整宽度滑块可以看到不同宽度下的表现差异。

CSS Justify
In the beginning there was nothing. Then, from that nothing, the first thought emerged—a tiny disturbance in the perfect void. It was not a sound, nor a light, but a possibility. And the possibility began to grow.

CSS justify 通过拉伸词间间距填充整行，在窄列下容易产生不均匀的"河流"（空白通道）。

Greedy + Hyphen
In the beginning there was nothing. Then, from that nothing, the first thought emerged—a tiny disturbance in the perfect void. It was not a sound, nor a light, but a possibility. And the possibility began to grow.

贪心算法在每行尽可能填词，到行尾断开。配合连字符可以减少剩余空间，但没有全局优化。

Knuth-Plass
In the beginning there was nothing. Then, from that nothing, the first thought emerged—a tiny disturbance in the perfect void. It was not a sound, nor a light, but a possibility. And the possibility began to grow.

Knuth-Plass 算法最小化全局"糟糕度"，使行间间距分布最均匀，有效消除河流。
